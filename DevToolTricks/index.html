Console Tricks

  • attribute modification

    • When you want to know whats causing something to change on the page! You can first inspect it. Click on the desired text. Then in the dev tools, where it is highlighted, right click t display the menu, click the break on selection and select attribute modification. that will activate a debugger. You can then activate the debugger by clicking on the page the desired text. (which is the ×BREAK×DOWN× word )
